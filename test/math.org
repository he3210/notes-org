皮尔逊相关度（Pearson）的公式如下：

\begin{equation}
\rho_{xy} =
\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i}y_{i} - \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i} \sum_{i=1}^{n}y_{i}}{n}}
{
\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i}^2 - \frac{(\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i})^2}{n}}
\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}y_{i}^2 - \frac{(\sum_{i=1}^{n}y_{i})^2}{n}}
}
\end{equation}
